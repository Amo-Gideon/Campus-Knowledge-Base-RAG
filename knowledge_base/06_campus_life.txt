CAMPUS LIFE AND STUDENT ACTIVITIES

Student life at AAMUSTED is vibrant and engaging. We have over 30 student clubs and organizations, including:
- The Developers Student Club (DSC), powered by Google.
- The Robotics and AI Club.
- The Environmental Sustainability Club.

Sports and Recreation:
The university has facilities for football, basketball, volleyball, and a state-of-the-art gym. Inter-faculty competitions are held every semester.

Accommodation:
There are five on-campus hostels (three for males, two for females) and numerous accredited off-campus housing options. The annual fee for an on-campus dormitory is GHS 1,200.
