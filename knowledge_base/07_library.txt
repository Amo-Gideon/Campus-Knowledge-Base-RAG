JOMO KENYATTA MEMORIAL LIBRARY

The main university library is a central hub for learning and research.

Operating Hours:
- Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Saturday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Sunday: 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Resources:
- Over 100,000 physical books and periodicals.
- Access to online journals including IEEE Xplore, ACM Digital Library, JSTOR, and SpringerLink.
- 24/7 access to the digital library portal for registered students and staff.
- 10 group study rooms, which can be booked online.

The library code of conduct requires silence in designated quiet zones.
