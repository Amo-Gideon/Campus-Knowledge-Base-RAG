LEADERSHIP: OFFICE OF THE CHANCELLOR

Chancellor: Dr. Nana Abena Mensah, Ph.D.
Tenure: 2022 - Present

Biography: Dr. Mensah is a renowned computer scientist and former industry executive with over 20 years of experience in technology and educational leadership. She holds a Ph.D. in Computational Linguistics from MIT and is a strong advocate for women in STEM.

The Chancellor provides strategic direction and oversight for the university's academic and administrative functions. She chairs the University Council.
