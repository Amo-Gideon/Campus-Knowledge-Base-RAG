FACULTIES AND DEPARTMENTS

AAMUSTED is organized into three main faculties:

1. Faculty of Computing and Engineering
   - Department of Computer Science
   - Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
   - Department of Electrical and Renewable Energy Engineering
   - Department of Civil Engineering

2. Faculty of Applied Sciences and Mathematics
   - Department of Biotechnology
   - Department of Environmental Science
   - Department of Mathematics and Statistics

3. Faculty of Education and Development Studies
   - Department of Curriculum and Teaching Studies
   - Department of Educational Leadership
   - Department of Psychology and Human Development
