ADMISSIONS PROCESS

Requirements for Undergraduate Programs:
- Completed application form via the AAMUSTED online portal.
- Senior Secondary School Certificate (SSSCE) or West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) with credits in core English, core Mathematics, and core Science, plus three electives.
- International students must provide equivalent qualifications translated into English.

Application Deadlines for the 2025/2026 Academic Year:
- Early Decision: January 31, 2025
- Regular Decision: March 31, 2025
- Late Applications: May 31, 2025 (subject to space availability)

Tuition Fees (Per Semester):
- Ghanaian Students: GHS 2,500
- International Students: USD 3,200
Financial aid and scholarships are available for qualified students.
