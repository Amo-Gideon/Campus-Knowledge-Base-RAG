ABOUT AAMUSTED

Established in 2018, AAMUSTED has quickly grown into a premier institution for STEM and Education in the region. We are recognized by the National Accreditation Board (NAB) and offer a unique curriculum designed to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

Our core values are:
- Integrity and Ethics
- Innovation and Excellence
- Diversity and Inclusion
- Community and Sustainability

The university is situated on a 200-acre campus in the serene environment of Kumasi, providing a perfect atmosphere for learning and research.
